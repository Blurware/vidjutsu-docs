---
title: "Import TikTok and Instagram references"
description: "Discover public TikTok or Instagram references and their metadata."
---

VidJutsu imports public TikTok and Instagram references. It does not provide ad-library, X, YouTube, Reddit, Google, or LinkedIn scraping.

Scrape returns discovery data and the provider's raw source media URLs. It does not stage media. When a downstream call needs a durable VidJutsu-hosted file, call the single-video download endpoint on one video at a time.

```bash
vidjutsu scrape instagram-post \
  --url "https://www.instagram.com/reel/example/"
```

The supported operations are TikTok profile, profile videos, video, transcript, comments, user search, and trending; and Instagram profile, posts, reels, post, and comments. All share the `scrape` rate group.

## Continue the workflow

1. Import a TikTok or Instagram reference, downloading media if you need a VidJutsu-hosted URL.
2. Pass the resulting URL to `watch`, `transcribe`, `check`, `overlay`, or `disclaimer`.
3. Poll the returned job with `mediaJobs.get` (SDK) or `vidjutsu status` (CLI) until it completes.
