## How it works

1. **Top up credits** — `POST /v1/api_keys` with your email. Pay with any card via Stripe.
2. **Get your API key** — Poll `GET /v1/api_keys/status` after payment. Your key is returned in the response.
3. **Analyze your content** — Call `/v1/critic`, `/v1/breakdown`, or `/v1/score` on any public video URL.
4. **Store and organize** — Create accounts and posts as data records (0 credits). Tag them for filtering.
