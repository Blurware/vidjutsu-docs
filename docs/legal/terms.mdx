VidJutsu is a developer API that performs automated analysis of audiovisual content and text against a stored corpus of platform policy rules. The **Compliance** endpoints specifically scan inputs against rules ingested from publicly-published platform documentation (YouTube Community Guidelines, TikTok Community Guidelines + ad policies, Instagram / Meta Community Standards, Facebook ad standards) and return a risk score with citations.
