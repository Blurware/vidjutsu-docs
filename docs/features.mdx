## Niche research

Your agent studies what's working in your niche — hooks, formats, topics. Every post is based on real data, not guesswork.

## 2 posts a day

Every day. Your page stays active and consistent without you doing anything.

## The learning loop

Performance data feeds back into research. What gets clicks shapes what gets made next. The page improves every cycle.

## Human account manager

A US-based account manager creates and posts on the account. Real person, not a bot — your account is less likely to get flagged.

## Content generation

Videos matched to what research says works. Not random — targeted to your niche. Choose from UGC, animation, slideshows, or clips.

## Link in bio tracking

Every click to your link is tracked. You see exactly how much traffic your page sends.

## Post-level analysis

Every post is analyzed after it goes live. Views, clicks, and engagement are tracked and fed back into the loop.

## Account warming

New accounts warm for up to 7 days before posting starts. The page reaches the right audience from day one.

## Composable

Use the full stack or just parts. Bring your own videos and use VidJutsu for QA, posting, and analytics. Every endpoint works on its own.
